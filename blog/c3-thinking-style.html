June 28 2015

My style of learning

Apparently I am a multi nodal Kinesthetic thinking style and a Concrete Random Abstract Sequential learner.

What this means is that I am a highly hand-ons learner. I like to get on my hands and knees and plunge my arms into the mess shoulder deep with the stuff splashing on my face.

I love that Dev Bootcamp does have a lot of introspection and how to contemplate our personal styles and how that can play with other styles of learning.

I think the best thing about this style of teaching is the whole "Embrace Confusion" mantra, because I am nothing if not confused most of the time. Honestly!

Oh and that's another thing, honesty! We must be honest, and strip away pretension and give kind, actionable and specific feedback to one another. Awesome stuff, if you ask me.

With regards to a specific action plan, I think I might just stay the course; it seems to be working for me so far. Also yoga. Yoga has been really helpful for me during Phase 0.

Finally, I think I can be perfectly honest and say I need to be expanding the growth mindset a lot more, perhaps not being so hard on myself on not getting some of the challenges right off the bat, and reaching out sooner to the support structures in place - I mean, that's what they're there for after all. Check you ego at the door!

Cheers!
